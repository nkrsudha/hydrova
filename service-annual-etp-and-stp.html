Annual Maintenance Contract ETP & STP

Hydrova offers specialized service packages which covers the water & waste water equipment with the most trusted people from the Industry. We follow preventive maintenance concept which helps in delivering value to customer. With the regular AMC package, we include scheduled inspection visits and scheduled maintenance and services. Also, there is a provision to opt for our value added services like monitoring, Lab and Instrument support by customizing this package.

Effluent Treatment Plants
Sewage Treatment Plants
Reverse Osmosis Plants
Softener Plants

Scope Of Work

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE VISITS

  • Ensuring smooth running of plant and also ensuring clear and odorless treated water from plant.
  • We conduct on site physical and chemical testing for raw water & treated water and also we check its flow rates.
  • To check all electrical circuits and parts.
  • To check the membrane parts.
  • To check the pumps and motors
  • To check valves and pipelines.
  • To check the electronic control panel.
  • To check the pressure gauge & pressure switches.
  • To wash / replace the micron filters.
  • To do PSF & ACF Filters Back Wash & Rinse.
  • To do monthly servicing of dosing system.
  • To give proper training to the plant operator.

SERVICE VISITS

Once in a year Filter Media Cleaning and Refilling, if required.

COMPLAINT VISIT

As and when some compliant arises in the unit, a service Engineer will make a visit within 48 hours of complaint login time. In the visit we will find the route cause for system break down and solve it. Replacing all broken & damaged spares immediately.
